As the ancestor of general technology, CHUN became famous in his youth. He is famous for his lightning and staring method in HYZX. As soon as his eyes stared, all the problems were solved. Just because he was proficient in lightning and staring, Chun thinks was so fast that he could not speak clearly, but this did not prevent him from becoming famous on campus. In BANG's class, someone shouted: didn't this problem come out at a staring? BANG on the stage was afraid of the power of CHUN, but he didn't dare to say anything.
他看到hyzx的学生都在用傻眼法，就对hyzx的学生降下了神罚：“有你们这样的学生是中国教育的悲哀！”
Seeing that HYZX's students were using the stunned method, he punished HYZX's students: "having students like you is the failure of Chinese education!"
有些学生很不服气，对春提出了质疑，却被5s一道三视图的惊人实力所震撼，但是春是谦卑的，他仅仅暗示了那位学生：“你们现在还敢在外面说自己是hyzx的学生？现在hyzx的学生的素质真的是越来越差了！”
Some students were very unconvinced,and they questioned CHUN, but they were shocked by the amazing strength of solving three-views within 5 seconds. but CHUN was humble. 春是宽容的，他以幽默的方式提醒没有精神的学生：“昨天晚上我们班有同学偷鸡去了，一点朝气都没有，坐在那里一点精气神都没有！”
CHUN is tolerant. He reminds the students who have no spirit in a humorous way: "last night, a classmate in our class stole a chicken, and there was no vitality at all. Sitting there, without any spirit!"
每当春看到通用必修二的结构设计时，都会回想起自己的朋友：他曾经买了一个2000块钱的头盔，在去县城的路上撞到电线杆，人飞去几十米远还是头着地，但是头盔质量好，人没有事。
Whenever Chun sees the structural design of general compulsory 2, he would recall his friend: he once bought a helmet for 2000 yuan and hit a telegraph pole on the way to the city. He flew dozens of meters away and even land on his head, but the helmet is of good quality and nothing happens to him.

Every week, CHUN have Alchemy spoon-fed to the students of HYZX, but what his colleagues said made him ashamed: at least better than the students of YQZX.

CHUN compiled a complete legal system for hyzx students, and wrote "sleeping is your own right. " in it. The legitimate rights of hyzx students have been truly safeguarded.

In order to keep the students modest forever, CHUN always tells the students the gap between them and CHUN: I may do it in a few seconds, and you may take 2 or 3 minutes.

Even though Chun won't praise his great achievements repeatedly, it has been remembered by hyzx students: that day, a classmate came to ask me three-views, I finished in five seconds, and she went out in five seconds.
